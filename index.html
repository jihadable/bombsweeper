How to play

  1. Objective: The main objective in Minesweeper is to clear the entire field without clicking on cells that contain bombs.
  2. Left-click on a cell to reveal its content. If the cell contains a bomb, the game ends.
  3. Right-click on a cell to mark it with flag as potentially containing a bomb. This helps you keep track of bomb locations.
  4. Numbers in Cells: The numbers displayed inside cells are clues indicating how many bombs are in the adjacent cells (including diagonals).
